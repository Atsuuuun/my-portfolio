Designer

Among designers, I want to be a designer who places emphasis on communication.
I want to be a designer who can solve clients' problems with designs created through communication.


私はデザイナーの中でもコミュニケーションに重きを置くデザイナーになりたいと考えております。

This is

My future

As a designer, I want to not only solve problems for many clients,
but also create an environment where the people I work with enjoy their work and are happy to work with me.
To achieve this, I would like to work on improving my design skills first.
